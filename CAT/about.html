MISSION OF SYSTEM

Convenience and Accessibility: Enable customers to order food anytime, anywhere, using mobile devices or computers, reducing the need for phone calls or physical visits to restaurants.

Efficiency and Accuracy: Streamline the ordering process to minimize errors and delays, ensuring orders are transmitted accurately and promptly to restaurant staff.


WHAT DO WE DO

Customer Interface: Customers access the food ordering system via a website or mobile app, browse menus, select items, and customize orders (e.g., toppings, quantity).
